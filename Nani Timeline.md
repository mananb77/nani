# **Krishna Bhargava \- Life Journey Timeline**

**September 7th, 1940 \- December 4th, 2021**

---

## **🌟 Early Life (1940s)**

### **1940 \- Birth**

* **Born:** September 7th, 1940 in Bikaner, Rajasthan  
* **Special Day:** Born on Janmashtami (Krishna's birthday)  
* **Family:** Youngest and most pampered of 4 siblings (3 sisters, 1 brother)  
* **Father:** Shankarlayal Bhargava (Doctor)  
* **Heritage:** From a well-educated family of doctors and army fighters

### **Childhood in Bikaner**

* Grew up like a **princess** with royal treatment  
* Had an Ambassador car  
* Family photos displayed in the museum of Bikaner to this day  
* Family had the first pressure cooker \- friends came to learn how to use it  
* Was embarrassed that people dropped food off for her

---

## **🎓 Education & Youth (1950s-1960s)**

### **Academic Achievement**

* Attended an all-girls school  
* Earned **Master's degree in Hindi & Sanskrit**  
* Achieved this during a time when girls were **discouraged from education**  
* Came from a well-educated family \- a rarity for women at the time

### **Athletic Excellence**

* **State Field Hockey Team** member  
* **Table Tennis State Champion**  
* Received coaching from **Dhyanchand** \- the famous hockey player  
* Signature style: Looking one direction, then hitting the ball another direction  
* Beat everyone in badminton  
* Known as the **Best Player**
